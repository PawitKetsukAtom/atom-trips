🧥 Layering Guide

Mountain Days (D4, D5)
Layer 1 (Base): T-shirt / thermal
Layer 2 (Mid): Fleece / hoody
Layer 3 (Outer): Windproof jacket
+ Beanie + neck gaiter + gloves

Ijen midnight = 2-5°C → all 3 layers
Bromo pre-dawn = 5-10°C → 2-3 layers
After sunrise → peel off layers!

⚡ Reminders & Tips

⚠️ Critical Reminders

  • 1. Down jacket — Ijen+Bromo 5-10°C + wind
  • 2. Headlamp — Ijen midnight hike
  • 3. Buff/mask — Ijen sulfur + Bromo dust
  • 4. Hiking shoes — all hiking locations
  • 5. Passport — Java→Bali flight
  • 6. Dry bag — Tumpak Sewu + Madakaripura

🛑 Safety Reminders

  • Ijen: toxic sulfur gas, follow guide
  • Bromo: no railing at crater edge
  • Nusa Penida: deadly cliffs, no photo worth dying for
  • Travel insurance with adventure sports cover

💡 Tips

  • Ijen: breathe through cloth, gas mask available
  • Bromo: book jeep through hotel
  • Nusa Penida: book fast boat in advance (Eka Jaya/Maruti)
  • Bali: Grab/Gojek everywhere
  • Money: withdraw IDR at SUB airport
  • SIM: Telkomsel at SUB airport
  • May = dry season, minimal rain
